Engagement - Form

Questions:

1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The `action` attribute in a `form` tag specifies where to send the form data when submitted.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

The `method` attribute in a `form` tag controls how the form sends data, using `"GET"` (adds data to the URL) or `"POST"` (hides data).

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

GET adds form data to the URL, making it visible and good for simple requests.
POST hides form data in the request body, making it safer for sensitive information.
