What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags start on a new line and take up the full width, while inline tags stay in the same line and only take up as much space as needed.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

  • Margin: Space outside the element, pushing it away from other elements.
  • Border: The edge of the element, surrounding its content and padding.
  • Padding: Space inside the element, between the content and the border.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

CSS units can be absolute (e.g fixed, like px, cm) or relative (e.g changes based on screen or parent, like %).

When would you use a figure tag?

Use the figure tag to group images or media with a caption for better structure.
