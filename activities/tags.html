    5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

    OL(Ordered List): Creates a numbered list
    Example:
    1. Hong Kong
    2. Hawaii
    3. Korea

    UL(Unordered List): Creates a bulleted list
    Example:
    • Red
    • Green
    • Blue
