Structual Tags

HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER

What goes in the header?

It contains search bar, logo, company name, etc.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Nav is to help users navigate the website like Home, About or Contact.
Nav can go in the header.
Nav doesn't need to go to the header
Nav does contain links to another website.

What goes in Main?

It cantains heading, paragraph, etc.

What goes in the Footer?

Usually it appear at the bottom of a webpage.
It contains copyright information, contact details, navigation links, social media icons and back to top link.
