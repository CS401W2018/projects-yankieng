Ice cream is a frozen dairy sweet treat made from milk, cream, sugar, and sometimes eggs. It's mixed while freezing to make it smooth and creamy, and can have different flavors like vanilla, chocolate, or fruit. You can also add things like chocolate chips or nuts.
