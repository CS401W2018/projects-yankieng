Sushi VS Sashimi

  • Sushi: With rice
  • Sashimi: Without rice (Only Seafood)

I like sashimi more than sushi because I don't like the taste of the vinegar in the rice part of a sushi.

What's the difference between Sushi & Sashimi

Popular Types:

  • Sushi: Nigiri, Maki, Uramaki, Temaki
  • Sashimi: Salmon (sake), Tuna (maguro), Yellowtail (hamachi)

Cultural Notes:

  • Origins in Japan
  • Etiquette (like dipping the fish side, not rice, in soy sauce)
