Anya Forger is a young, pink-haired daughter in Spy x Family who has the secret ability to read minds. She is adopted by Loid Forger, a spy, and becomes part of his mission to maintain world peace. Despite her childish antics and love for peanuts, Anya is clever and often helps her family in unexpected ways. Her expressive reactions and adorable personality make her a fan favorite in the series.

Yor Forger is the mother in Spy x Family. By day, she works as a kind and polite civil servant, but in secret, she is a highly skilled assassin known as the "Thorn Princess." She marries Loid Forger as part of a fake family arrangement, unaware that he is actually a spy. Despite her deadly profession, Yor is warm-hearted, somewhat na√Øve, and deeply cares for her adoptive daughter, Anya. Her incredible strength, combat skills, and awkward yet lovable personality make her a standout character in the series.

Loid Forger, also known by his codename "Twilight," is the father of Spy x Family. He is a highly skilled and intelligent spy working for the organization "WISE", tasked with maintaining peace between rival nations. To complete his mission, he creates a fake family, adopting Anya and marrying Yor, unaware of their own hidden identities. Despite initially viewing the family as just part of his mission, Loid grows to genuinely care for them. He is resourceful, calm under pressure, and willing to do whatever it takes to protect his loved ones and fulfill his duty.
